🪐 Tiny Planet Builder
A fully interactive, visually rich tiny planet builder with the following features:

✨ Features
🌍 Rotating Spherical World

Realistic sphere projection using trigonometry — items are placed on the 3D surface
Auto-rotates continuously at a gentle speed
Drag to spin with momentum/inertia (flick and it coasts to a stop)
Items appear larger/smaller and fade based on depth (front vs. back of sphere)
Items on the back face are hidden automatically
🌳 Drag-and-Drop Items (7 types)

Tree 🌳, Pine 🌲, House 🏠, Castle 🏰, Cloud ☁️, Mountain ⛰️, Flower 🌸
Drag from the palette and drop anywhere on the planet surface
Click any placed item to remove it
Pre-built starter planet with 15 items already placed
🌙 Day/Night Toggle

Seamless transition between warm sunny day and deep indigo night
Stars twinkle at night with animated opacity
Moon appears at night, animated sun with rays during day
Night shadow overlay on planet surface
UI panels and sky gradient all adapt
🌤 Weather Overlays (4 modes)

Clear — bright, clean skies
Rain 🌧️ — animated rain streaks, cloud layer, grey sky
Snow ❄️ — drifting snowflakes with float animation
Storm ⛈️ — dark clouds, lightning flashes, heavy rain, dramatic darkening
📊 Right Panel Stats

Live item count by category
World Status (time, weather, temperature)
Planet Happiness meter — score grows as you add trees, flowers, buildings
